# Entity Relationship Design (ERD)  
## Core Banking Operations Portal (CBOP)

---

## 1. Core Entities

### USERS
- UserId (PK)
- Username
- Email
- PasswordHash
- Status
- CreatedDate

### ROLES
- RoleId (PK)
- RoleName

### USER_ROLES
- UserId (FK)
- RoleId (FK)

---

### CUSTOMERS
- CustomerId (PK)
- FullName
- DateOfBirth
- PAN (Encrypted)
- Aadhaar (Encrypted)
- MobileNumber
- RiskCategory
- Status

---

### ACCOUNTS
- AccountId (PK)
- CustomerId (FK)
- AccountType
- Balance
- Status
- CreatedDate

---

### TRANSACTIONS
- TransactionId (PK)
- AccountId (FK)
- TransactionType
- Amount
- Status
- CreatedBy
- CreatedDate

---

### TRANSACTION_APPROVALS
- ApprovalId (PK)
- TransactionId (FK)
- ManagerId (FK)
- Decision
- Remarks
- DecisionDate

---

### AUDIT_LOGS
- AuditId (PK)
- UserId
- Action
- EntityName
- OldValue
- NewValue
- Timestamp
- IPAddress

---

### DOCUMENTS
- DocumentId (PK)
- CustomerId (FK)
- FileName
- FilePath
- UploadedDate

---

## 2. Relationships

- USER ↔ ROLE (Many-to-Many)
- CUSTOMER → ACCOUNT (One-to-Many)
- ACCOUNT → TRANSACTION (One-to-Many)
- TRANSACTION → APPROVAL (One-to-One)
- CUSTOMER → DOCUMENT (One-to-Many)
